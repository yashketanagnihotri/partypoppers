About Us

A Tech Start-Up

We are an upcoming tech startup with the intention to offer the best best partying experience that the customers have ever seen and that is not even the best part, we will offer highest quality service at very reasonable prices. Now that does call for a party.

Business Model

We will work on the concept of dropshipping, ie we will have local entrepreneurs and we will give them customers without actually entering into any of the businesses. The biggest challenge in this model will be making the services and food quality standardized in all cities where we will be operating.
